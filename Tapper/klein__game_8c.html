The game function file for the Tapper Game.

This is a version of the classic videogame Tapper developed by Marvin Glass and Associates in 1983. This version of Tapper is played using a serial terminal connection and an MSP430F5529. The goal of the game is to play as the barkeep who serves glasses of rootbeer to patrons as they come down the three bars and collect their empty glasses that they slide back to you. You can only hold three rootbeers at once and can have up to nine thirsty patrons at a time.
