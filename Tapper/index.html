Terminal Tapper!

A return of the hit classic Tapper developed by Marvin Glass and Associates in 1983. This version of Tapper is played using a serial terminal connection and an MSP430F5529. The goal of the game is to play as the barkeep who serves glasses of rootbeer to patrons as they come down the three bars and to collect their empty glasses that they slide back to you.

An embedded processor game by Nicholas Klein

Controls

  • Use the < and > keys to move up and down, alternatively the w and s keys can be used to move up and down.
  • Space will send a stored rootbeer down the bar to a patron
  • Moving down when at the bottom bar will refill your held glasses
